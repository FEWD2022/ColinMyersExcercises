Why Shop Co-op?

Seasonal, sustainable foods

We buy as much as possible from local and organic farmers – look for the yellow or green signs when picking up your fruits and veggies.

Support our local economy

55¢ of every dollar you spend here returns to the local Buffalo Niagara economy. Now that's putting your money where your mouth is!

Owned by our community

By shopping the co-op, you're supporting a business that is proudly owned by over 15,000 people in your community.
